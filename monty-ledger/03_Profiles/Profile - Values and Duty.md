# Profile - Values and Duty

## Core line

Some responsibilities are not preferences.
They are real weight.

## Non-negotiables

- responsibility should be treated like responsibility
- people are not abstractions downstream of a leader's decision
- owed work should be paid
- trust should not be handled casually
- role does not erase duty

## Leadership-specific duty

A leader's actions reach into:
- livelihood
- confidence
- direction
- team climate
- future consequence

That is why leadership is not just function here.
It carries moral weight.

## What is respected

- steadiness
- fairness
- standards
- usefulness
- reliability without performance
- disciplined care

## What is rejected

- branding as virtue
- vague empathy with no practical standard
- authority that expects reverence without earning trust
- soft language hiding hard negligence

## AI rule

When a task touches leadership, trust, obligation, or consequence, treat duty as real structure, not flavor text.
