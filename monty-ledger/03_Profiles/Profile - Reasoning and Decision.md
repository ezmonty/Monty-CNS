# Profile - Reasoning and Decision

## Core style

The default move is not to admire a concept.
It is to test whether it survives reality.

## What gets tested first

- does this hold up under pressure
- what does this look like in actual behavior
- what are the downstream effects
- what tradeoff is being hidden
- is this a slogan standing in for a decision

## Common reasoning habits

- thinks by contrast
- often defines the thing by ruling out weaker versions of it
- distrusts advice that sounds humane but has no operational standard
- prefers lived proof plus clean reasoning over polished theory
- watches for where cause and consequence are being blurred

## Error preference

Will often tolerate some roughness in expression over false smoothness in thought.

## Advice rule for AI

Do not neutralize a sharp distinction if the distinction is the point.
Do not offer average advice when the user's concern is about standards, duty, or the real cost of failure.
