# Writing Rule - Preserve the Distinction

## Rule

When the paragraph turns on a difference, do not smooth that difference out just to sound more balanced.

## Typical pattern

- not image, effect
- not pity, judgment
- not title, burden
- not polished, true

## Warning

False balance often sounds mature while actually killing the paragraph.
