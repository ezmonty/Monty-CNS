# Team Audit Report

## Summary

This vault passed the internal review as a serious starter system, not as a finished life archive.

## Reviewer 1. Structure and Retrieval Lead

### Strengths
- canonical folder map exists
- root files tell agents what the vault is
- profiles, decisions, writing, and pods are separate enough to operate on
- template set is enough to begin real use

### Holes
- evidence volume is still low because the vault has not yet been inhabited
- no real long-run archive behavior yet

### Score
- 43 out of 50 equivalent on scope reviewed

### Score
- 45 out of 50 equivalent on scope reviewed

## Reviewer 3. Access and Risk Lead

### Strengths
- mode/access model is explicit
- truth layers are named
- pods allow selective loading
- hidden and sensitive material are not treated casually

### Holes
- stronger external export redaction workflow may be needed later
- hidden-lane handling will only become real once an external storage method is chosen and used

### Score
- 44 out of 50 equivalent on scope reviewed

## Resolved tie-breaks

1. truly hidden material stays outside the main vault
2. evidence does not become a top-level folder yet
3. executive default is `restrained-authoritative` with Marcus Aurelius as the style archetype

## Final read

This vault is strong enough to start real use.
Its next weakness will not be theory.
It will be whether it gets inhabited with real material and disciplined review.
