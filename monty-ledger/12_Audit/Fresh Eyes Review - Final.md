# Fresh Eyes Review - Final

## Method
This review was run as if three fresh reviewers were handed the package late in the cycle and told to look for weakness, overdesign, drift, or fake depth.

## Reviewer A. System Architect
### What held
- the vault has a clear spine
- root docs tell both humans and agents how to behave
- folders, templates, and profiles are coherent
- the package is machine-readable without becoming lifeless

### What almost failed
- hidden-layer policy was still too loose until the outside-the-vault decision was locked
- executive tone needed a more precise archetype than plain authority

### Score
- architecture: 9/10
- retrieval clarity: 8/10
- operational readiness: 8/10

## Reviewer B. Identity and Voice Auditor
### What held
- the vault does not flatten raw, working, and output layers
- quote provenance is part of the method
- the writing stance mostly sounds like the system it is trying to produce

### What almost failed
- some earlier phrasing was still too framework-clean and not enough like an inhabited field
- more real evidence will still be needed for the profile to deepen honestly

### Score
- voice fit: 8/10
- self-layer integrity: 9/10
- anti-generic stance: 8/10

## Reviewer C. Adversarial Risk and Leakage Auditor
### What held
- sensitive material is not casually sprayed through the system
- pods and access levels make selective loading possible
- export discipline is explicit

### What almost failed
- without an external hidden-lane rule, the system could have become reckless
- without a clear executive mode, pain could leak into shark-facing outputs

### Score
- secrecy hygiene: 9/10
- leak control: 8/10
- threat awareness: 8/10

## Composite score
- 84/100

## Interpretation
This is strong enough to use as a real operating starter pack.
It is not a finished life archive, and it should not pretend to be.
Its main remaining weakness is not design. It is the need for more real evidence, more lived decisions, and more actual use.

## Approved changes from this review
- hidden lane kept outside main vault
- shadow reference template added
- executive pod tightened to restrained-authoritative with Marcus Aurelius as archetype
- security folder added for policy and setup guidance

## Deferred changes
- no automatic evidence top-level split yet
- no extra plugin complexity yet
- no hidden-lane automation until a real external storage choice is implemented
