# Decision - Preserve Raw and Output Layers

## Context

The system cannot treat every polished statement as the deepest truth.
It also cannot treat every raw statement as the best statement.

## Reasoning

The gap between raw, processed, and produced output is itself useful information.
That gap may reveal:
- strategy
- discipline
- pain management
- role control
- image management
- conflict
- maturity

## Chosen path

Every sensitive or important note type can track:
- access
- mask level
- truth layer
- role mode
- quote provenance

## Review question

Is the hidden layer useful in the vault, or should the most sensitive material live outside it entirely?
