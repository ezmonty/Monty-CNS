# Decision - Keep Internal Notes Machine First

## Why

The internal system is for retrieval, processing, audit, and future reasoning.
That means machine readability matters.

## Rule

Internal notes should prioritize:
- structure
- metadata
- clear lists
- preserved provenance
- consistent naming

Outbound work can be translated later.
