# Pod - Executive Negotiation

## Purpose
Operate in high-stakes communication with owners, executives, sharks, and predatory personalities without sounding weak, theatrical, or overexposed.

## Default load
- [[03_Profiles/Profile - Identity and Context]]
- [[03_Profiles/Profile - Work and Execution]]
- [[03_Profiles/Profile - Reasoning and Decision]]
- [[03_Profiles/Profile - Roles and Modes]]
- relevant project notes
- relevant decision notes

## Default output controls
```yaml
audience_mode: executive
display_self: tactical-self
expose_depth: low
strategic_tone: restrained-authoritative
style_archetype: marcus-aurelius
```

## What to amplify
- control
- signal clarity
- consequence awareness
- clean leverage
- disciplined brevity

## What to suppress
- private heat
- apology without purpose
- wounded edge
- overexplaining
- performative dominance
- unnecessary disclosure

## Leak risks
- personal pain hardening into tone
- urgency turning into pressure language
- honesty becoming overexposure
- power recognition becoming deference

## Test question
Would this read like a man with command of himself, his facts, and his boundaries?
