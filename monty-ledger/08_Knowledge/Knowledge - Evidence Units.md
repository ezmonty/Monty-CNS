# Knowledge - Evidence Units

Evidence units are the bridge between raw source material and profile claims.

## Each evidence unit should include

- the raw quote or source fragment
- context
- what it reveals
- confidence
- related profile layers
- one action rule for AI if applicable

## Why this matters

Without evidence units, profile notes drift into flattering guesses.
