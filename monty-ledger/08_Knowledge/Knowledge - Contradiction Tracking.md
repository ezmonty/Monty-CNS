# Knowledge - Contradiction Tracking

A contradiction is not always a defect.
Sometimes it reveals:
- role shift
- time shift
- protection strategy
- emotional instability in one moment
- growth
- posture management

## Rule

Track contradiction first.
Reconcile only when needed.
