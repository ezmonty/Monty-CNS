# Leadership - Team Energy

One of the more serious lines in this system is that building and leading a team well may be one of the most rewarding things a person can do.

## Why

Because people hand over energy, trust, labor, time, and often part of their future.
A leader gets to shape where that goes.

## Good use of that energy

- toward worthwhile work
- with enough order that the work does not become chaos
- with enough respect that people are not ground down without reason
- with enough standards that the team remains real, not performative
