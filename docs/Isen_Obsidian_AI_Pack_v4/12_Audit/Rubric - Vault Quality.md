# Rubric - Vault Quality

Score each category 0 to 5.

## 1. Retrieval quality
Can the right note be found fast?

## 2. Metadata discipline
Are frontmatter and controlled fields used consistently?

## 3. Canonical structure
Is there a clear place for projects, profiles, decisions, evidence, and pods?

## 4. Voice integrity
Does the vault preserve the actual voice instead of flattening it into generic language?

## 5. Role and mask handling
Does the vault model role, access, mask level, and truth layer well enough?

## 6. Provenance integrity
Can important lines be traced from raw to cleaned to approved output?

## 7. AI readiness
Can an agent actually operate on the vault without guessing structure?

## 8. Practical friction
Is the system usable without becoming its own full-time project?

## 9. Sensitivity handling
Does the system avoid spraying high-sensitivity material into unrelated contexts?

## 10. Strategic value
Would this vault actually make future thinking, writing, and decisions better?

## Interpretation

- 0 absent
- 1 token
- 2 weak
- 3 usable
- 4 strong
- 5 excellent

## Pass standard

40 out of 50 minimum.
45 plus is strong.
