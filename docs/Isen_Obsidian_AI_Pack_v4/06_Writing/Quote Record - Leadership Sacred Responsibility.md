# Quote Record - Leadership Sacred Responsibility

## Raw quote

> you know leadership is a sacred responsibility, my action impact there futures, thier livilhood and the resultant train. so i have to take very seriously

## Cleaned quote

## Approved output quote

> leadership has always felt close to sacred to me because your decisions do not stop with you

## Why this matters

The cleaned and approved lines are not the same as the raw line.
The raw line carries force, haste, and live emphasis.
The approved line carries chosen public articulation.

Use all three intentionally.
