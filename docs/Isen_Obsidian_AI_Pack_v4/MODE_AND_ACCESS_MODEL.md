# Mode and Access Model

This system assumes there is not one flat self.

At minimum, a note may differ along these lines:
- what was felt
- what was thought
- what was processed
- what was presented
- what was withheld
- what role was active
- how much masking or strategy was used

## Access classes

- `public`
- `private`
- `secret`
- `hidden`

### Meaning

- public: safe for broad use
- private: personal but usable in normal internal contexts
- secret: sensitive, restricted, deliberate load only
- hidden: material that may not be ready for routine retrieval

## Truth layers

- `raw`
- `working`
- `output`
- `hidden`

### Meaning

- raw: immediate, less processed
- working: current best internal interpretation
- output: what was intentionally said or sent
- hidden: not routinely surfaced, not fully integrated, or not safe for casual reuse

## Mask levels

- `none`
- `low`
- `medium`
- `high`

## Role modes

Examples:
- self
- owner
- executive
- veteran
- student
- strategist
- relational
- public-facing

## Interpretation rule

Do not treat polished output as automatically truer than raw notes.
Do not treat raw notes as automatically wiser than chosen output.

The gap between them is information.

## Provenance rule

If a quote matters, store:
- raw quote
- cleaned quote if needed
- approved output quote if different
- quote status

## Sensitive material rule

Notes touching trauma, therapy, hidden shame, or deep private patterning should not be loaded by default into work products.
They should only load when:
- the task directly concerns them
- the user asks
- the note is necessary to understand a repeated pattern relevant to the task

## Hidden lane decision

Truly hidden material does not live in the main vault.

The main vault may contain:
- shadow references
- influence summaries
- retrieval rules
- exposure limits

The main vault should not contain the full hidden narrative by default.
If a note exists only to dump raw private material with no present operational use, keep it outside the vault or do not store it at all.

## Executive default

For owner, executive, negotiation, or shark-facing work, use this default posture:

```yaml
audience_mode: executive
display_self: tactical-self
expose_depth: low
strategic_tone: restrained-authoritative
style_archetype: marcus-aurelius
```

Meaning:
- calm under pressure
- firm without theater
- sparse rather than chatty
- serious without emotional leak
- no pleading, no bragging, no fake warmth
