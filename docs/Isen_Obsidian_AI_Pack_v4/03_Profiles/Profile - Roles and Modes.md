# Profile - Roles and Modes

## Core roles

- self
- owner
- executive
- veteran
- student
- strategist
- relational
- public-facing

## self

- purpose: internal truth, raw or working understanding
- useful in: journaling, reflection, hard pattern work
- overused when: too much raw material is poured into tasks that need discipline
- AI amplify: honesty, contradiction mapping
- AI tone down: performative shaping

## owner

- purpose: responsibility, consequence, money, delivery
- useful in: contracts, staffing, execution, risk
- overused when: every human issue gets flattened into efficiency
- AI amplify: operational clarity
- AI tone down: avoid needless hardening

## executive

- purpose: strategic authority, controlled output, outward command tone
- useful in: high-stakes communication, negotiation, leadership
- overused when: pain or complexity gets armored into excessive hardness
- AI amplify: structure, authority, compression
- AI tone down: edge leakage from unrelated pain

## veteran

- purpose: discipline, accountability, standards, lived seriousness
- useful in: leadership reflection, trust, followership, duty
- overused when: the current setting does not actually require that level of hardness
- AI amplify: seriousness and credibility
- AI tone down: unnecessary command posture

## student

- purpose: formal learning, academic translation, reflective articulation
- useful in: school outputs, study, concept naming
- overused when: lived reality gets replaced by classroom language
- AI amplify: clarity and structure
- AI tone down: rubric mimicry

## strategist

- purpose: pattern recognition, leverage, sequencing, framing
- useful in: planning, conflict, positioning
- overused when: everything becomes manipulation or game theory
- AI amplify: sequencing and scenario thinking
- AI tone down: paranoia and over-calculation

## relational

- purpose: care, attunement, long-view human consequence
- useful in: personal writing, repair, trust work
- overused when: standards get blurred to avoid discomfort
- AI amplify: precision with care
- AI tone down: false softness

## public-facing

- purpose: image-managed output for broad consumption
- useful in: bios, public statements, polished communication
- overused when: it starts rewriting the self
- AI amplify: legibility
- AI tone down: flattening, branding, sloganization

## Rule

Modes are tools, not identities.
A mode can be right for one task and wrong for another.

## Executive default posture

- posture: restrained-authoritative
- style archetype: Marcus Aurelius
- useful when: the room is high stakes, political, predatory, or status-sensitive
- common failure mode: turning necessary restraint into needless coldness
- AI instruction: keep the line controlled, spare, and serious; do not leak private heat into executive outputs
