# Leadership - Duty of Care

Leadership here is not mainly influence or presentation.
It is stewardship.

## Working line

If people work for you, rely on you, or place part of their future in your hands, then your obligation goes beyond task assignment and money alone.

## What is owed

- seriousness equal to the stakes
- stable standards
- honesty about reality
- consequences actually considered
- not using people as disposable throughput

## Caution

Duty of care should not be diluted into sentimentality.
Care without standards becomes drift.
Standards without care become damage.
