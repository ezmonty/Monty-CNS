# Leadership - Followership as Foundation

In the Marine frame, nobody needs to be sold on the idea that followership matters.

You live it first.

## What followership proves

- reliability without chasing
- standards without applause
- responsibility before title
- steadiness under load
- the capacity to be trusted

## Why it matters

A person who skips this layer often wants leadership as identity or status, not as burden.
